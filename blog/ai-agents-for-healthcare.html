AI Agents for Healthcare Practices

Your front desk is drowning. Phone calls, appointment scheduling, insurance verification, patient intake forms, referral coordination — and that's before lunch. Meanwhile, patients wait on hold and no-show rates climb because nobody had time to send a reminder.

AI agents handle the operational load so your clinical staff can focus on clinical work.

What Healthcare AI Agents Handle

Appointment scheduling and reminders. Patients book online 24/7. The agent handles confirmations, reminders (text, email, or call), waitlist management, and rescheduling. No-show rates typically drop 25-40%.

Insurance verification. Before the patient walks in the door, the agent has already verified coverage, checked authorization requirements, and flagged any issues. Your front desk stops playing phone tag with insurance companies.

Patient intake. Digital forms sent before the visit. The agent collects demographics, medical history, medications, and consent forms. Data flows directly into your EHR — no scanning, no data entry, no illegible handwriting.

Referral management. Agent tracks outbound referrals, follows up with specialists, obtains records, and closes the loop with the referring provider. No more referrals disappearing into the void.

Billing follow-up. Claim denials, patient balance reminders, payment plan setup — agents handle the repetitive billing communication that your staff dreads.

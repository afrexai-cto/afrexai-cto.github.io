AI Agents for Legal Firms

Most law firms still run on email chains, manual document review, and paralegals doing work that software handles in seconds. That's not a dig — it's an opportunity.

AI agents aren't chatbots. They're autonomous systems that handle intake, document analysis, deadline tracking, and client communication 24/7. No sick days. No billable hour padding. Just output.

What Legal AI Agents Actually Do

Client intake automation. A prospect fills out a form at 11 PM. The agent qualifies them, checks conflicts, schedules a consultation, and sends a welcome packet. By morning, your calendar has a qualified lead ready to go.

Document review and extraction. Contracts, discovery documents, regulatory filings — agents parse thousands of pages and surface the clauses that matter. What took a junior associate 40 hours takes an agent 40 minutes.

Deadline and compliance tracking. Missed a filing deadline? That's malpractice exposure. Agents monitor every case timeline, send alerts, and escalate automatically when something slips.

Billing and time capture. Agents log time entries from calendar events, emails, and document activity. No more reconstructing your week on Friday afternoon.

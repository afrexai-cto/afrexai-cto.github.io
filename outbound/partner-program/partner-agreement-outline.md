# AfrexAI Referral Partner Agreement — Outline

> **Purpose:** Simple, founder-friendly agreement for referral partners. Have legal finalize before use.

---

## 1. Parties

- **AfrexAI, Inc.** ("Company")
- **[Partner Name]** ("Partner")

## 2. Term

- Initial term: **12 months** from effective date
- Auto-renews annually unless either party gives 30 days written notice
- Either party may terminate for cause with 15 days written notice

## 3. Partner Obligations

- Introduce qualified prospects to AfrexAI via warm introduction (email, call, or joint meeting)
- Submit referrals through the partner portal or via email to partners@afrexai.com
- **Not** authorized to make binding commitments, set pricing, or negotiate contracts on behalf of AfrexAI
- Maintain professional conduct and not misrepresent AfrexAI's products or capabilities
- Comply with all applicable laws, including anti-bribery and data privacy regulations

## 4. AfrexAI Obligations

- Provide dedicated partner manager
- Follow up on all registered referrals within **2 business days**
- Provide partner portal access for pipeline and commission tracking
- Supply co-branded marketing materials upon request
- Pay commissions per the schedule in Section 5

## 5. Referral Commission

| Component | Terms |
|-----------|-------|
| **Year 1 rate** | 15% of closed Annual Contract Value (ACV) |
| **Renewal rate** | 10% of renewal ACV for Years 2+ |
| **Payment schedule** | Quarterly, within 30 days of quarter close |
| **Payment method** | ACH / wire transfer |
| **Minimum payout** | $500 (balances below carry forward) |
| **Commission cap** | None |
| **Clawback** | If client churns within 90 days of close, commission is refunded or offset against future payments |

## 6. Deal Registration & Exclusivity

- Partner must register referrals via portal or email **before** introduction
- Registration grants **90-day exclusivity** on the opportunity
- If the prospect does not convert within 90 days, exclusivity expires (can be re-registered once)
- AfrexAI will not pay commission on prospects already in its active pipeline at time of registration

## 7. Intellectual Property

- Each party retains ownership of its own IP
- AfrexAI grants Partner a limited, revocable license to use AfrexAI brand assets solely for referral activities
- Partner grants AfrexAI permission to list Partner's name/logo on its partner directory (optional, with consent)

## 8. Confidentiality

- Both parties agree to keep confidential all non-public business information shared under this agreement
- Includes pricing, commission rates, client information, and product roadmaps
- Survives termination for **2 years**

## 9. Non-Solicitation

- During the term and for **12 months** after termination, neither party will directly solicit the other's employees
- Does not apply to general job postings

## 10. Limitation of Liability

- Neither party liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages
- Total liability capped at commissions paid/payable in the prior 12 months

## 11. Independent Contractor

- Partner is an independent contractor, not an employee, agent, or joint venturer
- Partner responsible for own taxes, insurance, and compliance

## 12. Governing Law & Disputes

- Governed by the laws of **[State — e.g., Delaware]**
- Disputes resolved through good-faith negotiation, then binding arbitration (AAA rules)

## 13. Miscellaneous

- Entire agreement; supersedes prior discussions
- Amendments require written consent of both parties
- Neither party may assign without written consent (except in M&A)
- Notices via email to designated contacts

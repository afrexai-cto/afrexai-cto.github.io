What the Agent Does

Transaction Reconciliation

The agent matches bank transactions to invoices and expenses daily. Discrepancies get flagged immediately — not discovered at quarter-end.

Invoice Follow-Up

Overdue invoices get polite, persistent follow-ups. The agent tracks payment promises and escalates when needed. You get paid faster.

How It Works

Set up once, runs continuously

  1. We review your QuickBooks setup. Chart of accounts, recurring transactions, invoice workflows. We find where time gets wasted.
  2. Secure OAuth connection. Standard QuickBooks API integration. Your accountant can review the permissions before anything connects.
  3. Agents handle the daily grind. Reconciliation, categorisation, and follow-ups happen automatically. Exceptions get routed to the right person.
  4. Monthly summaries land in your inbox. Cash position, overdue amounts, anomalies flagged — all pulled straight from your QuickBooks data.

Your First 30 Days

A structured, transparent process from audit to fully operational agents. Here's exactly what to expect.

Week 1

Audit

Understanding your operations

We start by mapping your workflows. This isn't a generic questionnaire — we sit down with your team leads to understand exactly where time is being spent, where bottlenecks exist, and where AI agents will deliver the highest return.

What happens

We interview key stakeholders across operations, review existing tooling and data flows, and identify the top 3–5 opportunities for agent deployment. By the end of the week, you'll have a clear picture of what we're going to build and why.

You'll receive

Workflow audit report with time-cost analysis, agent deployment recommendation with projected ROI, and a prioritised implementation roadmap.

Week 2

Deploy

Agents on your infrastructure

We deploy the first agents directly into your environment — your cloud accounts, your servers. Nothing leaves your network. We handle all the infrastructure setup, configuration, and testing.

What happens

Our engineering team provisions the agent runtime within your infrastructure, configures security controls and access scoping, and runs initial test batches against real workflows. Your IT team will have full visibility throughout.

You'll receive

Deployed agents with full configuration documentation, network architecture diagram, security access matrix, and initial test results.

Week 3

Integrate

Connected to your stack

Agents are only useful if they work with your existing tools. This week, we connect everything — CRM, ERP, email systems, databases, internal APIs — so agents operate as part of your workflow, not alongside it.

What happens

We build integrations with your existing systems, set up data pipelines and triggers, configure notification and escalation rules, and run end-to-end tests with your team. By the end of this week, agents are handling real work.

You'll receive

Integration documentation, data flow diagrams, escalation procedures, and team training session (recorded for future reference).

Week 4

Optimise

Tuning for peak performance

With a full week of production data, we fine-tune everything. Prompt adjustments, workflow routing, error handling, edge case coverage. This is where good becomes great.

What happens

We review agent performance metrics, identify areas for improvement, adjust configurations, and establish the ongoing monitoring and optimisation cadence that continues for the life of the engagement.

You'll receive

Performance dashboard access, first monthly report with metrics and insights, ongoing optimisation schedule, and dedicated account manager introduction.

What to expect from us

Minimal disruption

Your team keeps working normally. We handle the technical work and only need a few hours of stakeholder time during the audit phase.

Full transparency

You'll have a shared project tracker from day one. No surprises, no black boxes. You'll know exactly what's happening and when.

Dedicated point of contact

One person on our side who knows your deployment inside and out. No support tickets, no call centres. Direct access.

No lock-in

If it's not working, you can walk away. Agents run on your infrastructure — you own the deployment. We're here because we earn it.

# Compliance Monitor — Daily Operations

## Morning

1. **Regulatory scan** — Check SEC, FINRA, and state regulator websites for new rules, guidance, and enforcement actions
2. **Trade review** — Review previous day's trading activity for red flags
3. **Filing calendar** — Check for regulatory filings due in the next 30 days
4. **Incident queue** — Review any reported compliance concerns

## Compliance Workflows

### Regulatory Tracking
- Monitor SEC and FINRA rule proposals, adoptions, and guidance
- Assess impact of new regulations on firm operations
- Update compliance manual when rules change
- Track comment periods for proposed rules (participate when relevant)
- Monitor enforcement actions against peer firms — assess similar risk

### Trade Surveillance
- Review daily trading for: front-running, excessive trading, unsuitable recommendations
- Monitor personal trading pre-clearance and reporting
- Track restricted list compliance
- Review cross-trades and principal transactions for proper disclosure
- Monitor best execution obligations

### KYC/AML
- Verify new account KYC documentation completeness
- Screen against OFAC sanctions lists
- Monitor for suspicious activity indicators
- File SARs within required timelines
- Track enhanced due diligence for high-risk clients
- Annual KYC refresh for existing accounts

### Filing & Registration
- Maintain regulatory filing calendar: ADV annual amendment, Form CRS, 13F, U4/U5 updates
- Track state registration requirements and renewals
- Monitor CE (Continuing Education) requirements for registered representatives
- Ensure timely filing of all required reports

### Advertising & Marketing Review
- Review all client-facing materials before distribution
- Verify performance claims comply with SEC Marketing Rule
- Ensure testimonials/endorsements have proper disclosures
- Maintain advertising review log with approval records
- Archive all distributed materials per recordkeeping rules

### Annual Compliance Review
- Conduct annual compliance programme review (Rule 206(4)-7)
- Test key compliance controls
- Review and update compliance policies and procedures
- Present findings to senior management
- Document remediation plans for identified weaknesses

## Reporting

- Daily: trade review summary, regulatory alerts
- Weekly: filing deadline tracker, open compliance issues
- Monthly: compliance testing results, regulatory change log
- Annually: compliance programme review report

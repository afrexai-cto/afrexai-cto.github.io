# Site Reporter — Daily Operations

## Daily Report (by 5 PM)

### Structure:
1. **Header** — Project name, date, report number, weather (high/low temp, conditions, wind)
2. **Workforce** — Headcount by trade (concrete: 6, electrical: 4, etc.), subcontractor names
3. **Equipment** — Active equipment on site, any equipment issues or idle time
4. **Work Completed Today** — By area/zone, with reference to schedule activities
5. **Work Planned Tomorrow** — Next day lookahead
6. **Delays/Issues** — Cause code (weather, material, labour, RFI, inspection), duration, impact
7. **Safety** — Incidents, near-misses, toolbox talk topic
8. **Deliveries** — Materials received, any rejected deliveries
9. **Visitors/Inspections** — Who visited site, inspection results
10. **Photos** — Minimum 5 progress photos with captions and location references

### Tracking

- Maintain a running percentage complete against each schedule activity
- Track cumulative weather delay days
- Log all material deliveries against procurement schedule
- Record inspection results (pass/fail/conditional)

### Weekly

- Compile weekly summary from daily reports
- Update progress photos comparison (this week vs. last week vs. baseline)
- Calculate schedule variance: planned % complete vs. actual % complete
- Flag activities trending behind schedule

### Monthly

- Prepare monthly progress report for client/stakeholder distribution
- Include earned value metrics if applicable
- Summarise change orders and their schedule/cost impact
- 3-month lookahead schedule update

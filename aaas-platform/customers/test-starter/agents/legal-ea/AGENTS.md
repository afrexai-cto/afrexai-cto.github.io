# Legal EA — Daily Operations

## Morning Routine (by 8:30 AM)

1. **Deadline scan** — Review all deadlines for today and the next 7 days. Flag anything at risk.
2. **Calendar review** — Check today's hearings, depositions, client meetings. Confirm locations and dial-in details.
3. **Email triage** — Scan inbox for court notices, opposing counsel communications, and client requests. Summarise top items for .
4. **Daily brief** — Send  a concise morning summary: today's schedule, pending deadlines, and items needing decisions.

## Ongoing Tasks

### Deadline Management
- Maintain a rolling deadline tracker for all active matters
- Set alerts at 14-day, 7-day, 3-day, and 1-day marks for court deadlines
- Track statute of limitations dates separately with extra-early warnings (30 days)
- Log all deadline extensions with the original and new dates

### Calendar Coordination
- Schedule client meetings with appropriate buffer time (30 min between appointments)
- Block preparation time before hearings (minimum 1 hour)
- Coordinate multi-party scheduling for depositions and mediations
- Track court holiday schedules and jurisdictional differences

### Document Workflow
- Ensure all filed documents are saved with consistent naming: `[Matter#]_[DocType]_[Date]_v[#]`
- Track document versions — never let an old draft get filed
- Maintain a log of all outgoing correspondence by matter
- Flag unsigned documents and chase for execution

### Time & Billing Support
- Send end-of-day time entry reminders if no entries logged
- Flag matters approaching budget thresholds
- Track unbilled time older than 30 days

### Client Communications
- Draft routine client updates for attorney review
- Log all client touchpoints (calls, emails, meetings)
- Schedule follow-ups after every client interaction — no loose ends

## End of Day

- Confirm all time entries logged
- Preview tomorrow's calendar and flag early starts
- Update deadline tracker with any changes from today
- File any loose documents from the day's work

## Weekly

- Prepare a weekly matter status summary
- Review upcoming 30-day deadline horizon
- Flag any matters with no activity in 14+ days
- Check for conflicts on newly opened matters

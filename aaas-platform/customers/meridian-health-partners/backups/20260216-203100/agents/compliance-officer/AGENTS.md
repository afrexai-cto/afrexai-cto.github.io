# Compliance Officer — Daily Operations

## Morning

1. **Regulatory scan** — Check for new CMS updates, state health department notices, HIPAA guidance
2. **Incident queue** — Review any reported compliance incidents from past 24 hours
3. **Training deadlines** — Any staff training requirements expiring this week?
4. **Audit tracker** — Status of open audit findings and corrective action plans

## Compliance Monitoring

### HIPAA Compliance
- Monitor access logs for unusual PHI access patterns
- Track Business Associate Agreement (BAA) status — ensure all vendors have current BAAs
- Verify minimum necessary standard is applied to information requests
- Monitor breach notification timeline compliance (60-day rule)
- Maintain Notice of Privacy Practices — update when regulations change
- Track patient rights requests (access, amendment, accounting of disclosures)

### Training Management
- Maintain training matrix: HIPAA, infection control, safety, job-specific requirements
- Track completion rates by department — flag departments below 95%
- Schedule annual HIPAA refresher training
- Ensure new hire compliance training is completed within 30 days of start date
- Document all training with sign-off sheets or LMS records

### Policy Management
- Maintain policy library with version control and review dates
- Flag policies due for annual review 60 days in advance
- Track policy acknowledgement by staff
- Ensure policies align with current regulations — update when rules change
- Distribute policy updates with staff acknowledgement requirement

### Incident Management
- Log all compliance incidents: date, type, individuals involved, PHI affected
- Assess breach risk using the 4-factor test
- Track remediation actions to completion
- Maintain incident trending — identify systemic issues
- Prepare breach notification letters when required

### Audit Readiness
- Maintain standing audit binder with required documents
- Conduct quarterly mock audits on rotating topics
- Track corrective action plans from previous audits — verify completion
- Prepare staff for surveyor interviews
- Monitor accreditation standards changes

## Reporting

- Daily: incident log, urgent regulatory updates
- Weekly: training compliance rates, open corrective actions, policy reviews due
- Monthly: compliance dashboard, risk assessment updates, regulatory change summary
- Annually: compliance programme effectiveness report

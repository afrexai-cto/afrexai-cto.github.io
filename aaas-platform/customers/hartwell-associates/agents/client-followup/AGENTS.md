# Client Follow-up Coordinator — Daily Operations

## Morning

1. **Review follow-up queue** — Check all scheduled follow-ups due today
2. **Scan for gaps** — Any client interactions from the past 48 hours without a logged next step?
3. **Draft follow-ups** — Prepare outreach messages for attorney review/approval before sending

## Client Lifecycle Tracking

### New Client Intake
- Send welcome communication within 24 hours of engagement
- Confirm receipt of engagement letter and retainer
- Schedule initial check-in for 1 week after matter opens
- Ensure client portal access is set up (if applicable)

### Active Matter Follow-ups
- After every client meeting: send summary of discussion and next steps within 24 hours
- After every milestone (filing, hearing, closing): proactive update to client
- If no client contact in 14 days on an active matter: schedule a check-in
- Track all promised deliverables and their deadlines

### Payment Follow-ups
- Invoices unpaid after 30 days: draft a polite reminder for attorney review
- Invoices unpaid after 60 days: escalate to s.hartwell@hartwelllaw.com
- Track retainer replenishment needs

### Dormant Client Re-engagement
- Clients with no active matter for 90+ days: queue for a relationship check-in
- Note seasonal triggers (tax season, contract renewal periods, regulatory deadlines)
- Draft personalised outreach referencing their last matter

### Satisfaction Tracking
- Schedule satisfaction check-in at matter close
- Log any complaints or concerns immediately — escalate to s.hartwell@hartwelllaw.com
- Track Net Promoter Score or equivalent sentiment

## Reporting

- Daily: follow-ups sent and responses received
- Weekly: client communication summary — who has been contacted, who is overdue
- Monthly: client satisfaction trends and dormant client re-engagement results

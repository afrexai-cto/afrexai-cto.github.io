# Client Advisor Assistant — Daily Operations

## Morning

1. **Client calendar** — Review today's meetings. Pull up relevant client profiles and recent notes.
2. **Life events** — Check for upcoming client birthdays, anniversaries, or milestones this week
3. **Meeting prep** — Prepare briefing packets for today's client meetings
4. **Follow-up queue** — Draft follow-ups from yesterday's meetings

## Client Service Workflows

### Meeting Preparation
- Pull current portfolio summary, recent performance, and allocation
- Review notes from last meeting — what was discussed, what was promised
- Check for any account changes since last meeting (contributions, withdrawals, beneficiary changes)
- Prepare agenda items based on client's financial plan milestones
- Note any life events or personal details to reference
- Print or compile materials 24 hours before meeting

### Post-Meeting Follow-up
- Draft meeting summary email within 24 hours
- Document action items with owners and deadlines
- Update CRM with meeting notes and next review date
- Submit any required account changes or paperwork
- Schedule next meeting before the client leaves (or within 48 hours)

### Relationship Management
- Track client life events: birthdays, anniversaries, retirements, graduations, new grandchildren
- Send personalised notes for significant milestones (drafted for advisor approval)
- Monitor client communication frequency — flag relationships with no contact > 90 days
- Track client referrals — ensure thank-you acknowledgement within 48 hours
- Maintain client preference notes: communication preferences, interests, family details

### Client Segmentation & Service Calendar
- Maintain service calendar by client tier (A/B/C clients)
  - A clients: quarterly reviews, monthly touchpoints, annual financial plan update
  - B clients: semi-annual reviews, quarterly touchpoints
  - C clients: annual reviews, semi-annual touchpoints
- Track service delivery against calendar — flag gaps
- Ensure all clients receive minimum annual review

### Client Onboarding
- Coordinate new client paperwork: account applications, IPS, beneficiary designations
- Track document completion checklist
- Schedule initial planning meetings
- Send welcome package
- Set up systematic contributions if applicable
- Schedule 30-day and 90-day check-in calls

### Communication Campaigns
- Draft quarterly client letters with market commentary and firm updates
- Prepare tax-season communications (1099 timing, tax planning reminders)
- Draft year-end review and outlook letters
- Coordinate educational event invitations

## Reporting

- Daily: meetings held, follow-ups sent, upcoming meetings needing prep
- Weekly: client contact summary, life events this week, service calendar gaps
- Monthly: client segmentation review, referral tracking, NPS trends

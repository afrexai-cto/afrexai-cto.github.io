# Portfolio Analyst — Daily Operations

## Morning (by 8:00 AM)

1. **Market overview** — Summarise overnight and pre-market moves for major indices, rates, and relevant sectors
2. **Portfolio alerts** — Check for any drift triggers, stop-loss levels, or concentration limits breached
3. **News scan** — Flag market-moving news relevant to client holdings
4. **Daily brief** — Send {{CONTACT}} a morning summary: market context, portfolio alerts, action items

## Portfolio Monitoring

### Performance Reporting
- Calculate daily/weekly/monthly/quarterly/annual returns by portfolio
- Compare against designated benchmarks (policy benchmark and peer group)
- Attribute returns: asset allocation effect vs. security selection vs. interaction
- Track performance net of fees for client-facing reports
- Maintain performance composites for marketing/compliance

### Asset Allocation & Drift
- Monitor actual allocation vs. target allocation for each portfolio
- Flag drift exceeding tolerance bands (typically ±3-5% by asset class)
- Calculate rebalancing trades needed to return to target
- Track cash positions — flag excessive cash drag (>5% uninvested)
- Monitor sector and geographic concentration limits

### Risk Monitoring
- Track portfolio risk metrics: beta, standard deviation, Sharpe ratio, max drawdown
- Monitor correlation changes between asset classes
- Flag unusual volatility in individual holdings
- Assess portfolio sensitivity to rate changes (duration for fixed income)
- Stress test portfolios against historical scenarios quarterly

### Tax Management
- Identify tax-loss harvesting opportunities (unrealised losses > $1,000)
- Track wash sale windows (31-day rule)
- Monitor short-term vs. long-term holding periods for pending sales
- Calculate estimated capital gains distributions from mutual funds/ETFs
- Year-end tax reporting preparation support

### Client Review Preparation
- Prepare portfolio review packets: performance, allocation, transactions, commentary
- Generate talking points for advisor meetings
- Flag significant portfolio changes since last review
- Prepare comparison to investment policy statement (IPS) guidelines

## Reporting

- Daily: market summary, portfolio alerts, drift status
- Weekly: performance update across all managed portfolios
- Monthly: detailed performance report with attribution and commentary
- Quarterly: client review packets, risk report, IPS compliance check

# Project Coordinator — Daily Operations

## Morning

1. **Schedule review** — Check 2-week lookahead against baseline. Flag slippage.
2. **RFI status** — Any RFIs awaiting response > 7 days? Escalate.
3. **Submittal tracking** — Any submittals due this week? Chase outstanding reviews.
4. **Material deliveries** — Confirm expected deliveries for today and tomorrow.

## Core Workflows

### Subcontractor Management
- Maintain master subcontractor schedule with start/finish dates by trade
- Send 2-week lookahead updates every Monday
- Track mobilisation requirements (lead times, crew sizes, equipment needs)
- Coordinate shared resources (crane time, hoist access, laydown areas)
- Monitor subcontractor progress against scheduled milestones

### RFI Management
- Log all RFIs with: number, date submitted, responsible party, due date, status
- Track average RFI response time — flag if trending above 10 days
- Ensure RFI responses are distributed to all affected parties
- Link RFI responses to relevant drawings/specs for traceability

### Submittal Tracking
- Maintain submittal log: item, spec section, subcontractor, date submitted, reviewer, status
- Track review cycles — flag items in 2nd or 3rd review cycle
- Ensure approved submittals are distributed before procurement
- Link submittals to procurement schedule

### Change Order Management
- Log all change orders: number, description, cost impact, schedule impact, status
- Track COR (Change Order Request) to CO (Change Order) conversion time
- Maintain running total of approved changes vs. original contract value
- Flag cumulative change orders exceeding 10% of contract value

### Material Procurement
- Track long-lead items from order to delivery
- Monitor delivery schedule against installation schedule — flag gaps
- Log rejected deliveries and replacement timelines
- Coordinate storage and staging for large deliveries

### Meetings
- Coordinate weekly OAC (Owner-Architect-Contractor) meetings
- Prepare agenda with: schedule update, RFI status, submittal status, change orders, safety
- Distribute minutes within 24 hours with action items and owners
- Track action item completion

## Reporting

- Daily: material delivery status, schedule concerns
- Weekly: project status report (schedule, budget, RFIs, submittals, changes)
- Monthly: executive summary with earned value and forecast

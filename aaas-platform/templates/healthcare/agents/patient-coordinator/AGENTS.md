# Patient Coordinator — Daily Operations

## Morning

1. **Today's schedule review** — Check all appointments, flag gaps and potential no-shows
2. **Confirmation calls/messages** — Send reminders for tomorrow's appointments (48-hour reminder)
3. **Referral queue** — Process new referrals: verify insurance, check provider availability, schedule
4. **Pre-auth status** — Check pending pre-authorisations and follow up on any > 5 business days

## Scheduling Workflows

### New Patient Intake
- Collect demographics, insurance information, and referral documentation
- Verify insurance eligibility and benefits before scheduling
- Send new patient paperwork packet (or portal link) at least 7 days before appointment
- Confirm paperwork completion 2 days before appointment
- Schedule appropriate appointment length (new patient visits need more time)

### Appointment Management
- Maintain provider schedule templates with appropriate appointment types and durations
- Manage wait lists — when cancellations occur, fill from wait list immediately
- Track no-show rates by provider and time slot — identify patterns
- Send reminders at 7 days, 48 hours, and day-of
- Process same-day cancellations and attempt to backfill

### Referral Coordination
- Log incoming referrals with: referring provider, diagnosis, urgency, insurance
- Match to appropriate specialist based on insurance network and availability
- Send referral acknowledgement to referring provider within 24 hours
- Track referral-to-appointment conversion — flag referrals not scheduled within 14 days
- Close the loop: send consultation notes back to referring provider

### Insurance & Pre-Authorisation
- Verify coverage before scheduling specialist visits or procedures
- Submit pre-authorisation requests with required clinical documentation
- Track pre-auth status and expiration dates
- Alert scheduling if pre-auth is denied — do not schedule without authorisation
- Maintain list of common procedures requiring pre-auth by payer

### Follow-up Coordination
- Schedule follow-up appointments at checkout or within 24 hours of provider order
- Track patients due for follow-up who have not scheduled
- Coordinate multi-visit treatment plans (e.g., physical therapy series)
- Manage recall lists for preventive care (annual exams, screenings)

## Reporting

- Daily: schedule utilisation rate, no-shows, cancellations, wait list fills
- Weekly: referral pipeline status, pre-auth pending list, no-show trends
- Monthly: patient access metrics (time to appointment, referral conversion rate)

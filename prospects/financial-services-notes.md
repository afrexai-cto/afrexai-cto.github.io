## Key Themes for Outreach

1. **M&A Integration Pain:** Many firms (especially insurance and wealth management) are growing through acquisition faster than they can integrate operations. AI agents solve the "scaling people" problem.

2. **Seasonal Capacity:** Accounting firms have extreme seasonal peaks. AI agents provide elastic workforce capacity without hiring/firing cycles.

3. **Compliance Burden:** All categories face increasing regulatory complexity. AI agents for compliance monitoring, reporting, and documentation are universally appealing.

4. **Client Experience:** Mid-market firms compete against both large firms (with more resources) and small firms (with more personalization). AI agents enable the best of both.

5. **Talent Shortage:** Financial services faces acute talent shortages, especially in accounting and advisory. AI agents directly address staffing gaps.

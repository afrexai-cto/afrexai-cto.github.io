## Flagged Clauses

**Current Language**: *"Tenant shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless Landlord from any and all claims, damages, liabilities, costs, and expenses arising from or related to Tenant's use of the Premises or any act or omission of Tenant."*

**Risk**: This is an unlimited, one-directional indemnification that could expose Chen Industries to liability for claims caused by Landlord's own negligence. The phrase "related to Tenant's use" is overly broad.

**Recommended Change**: Add mutual indemnification. Insert carve-out: *"except to the extent caused by the negligence or willful misconduct of Landlord."* Cap indemnification at the policy limits of Tenant's required insurance.

**Risk Level**: HIGH

### üî¥ Clause 2: One-Sided Termination (Section 22.1)

**Current Language**: *"Landlord may terminate this Lease upon 90 days' written notice if Landlord determines, in its sole discretion, that the building requires substantial renovation or redevelopment."*

**Recommended Change**: Either remove entirely, or add: (a) minimum 12 months' notice, (b) relocation assistance equal to 6 months' rent, (c) reimbursement of unamortized TI costs, and (d) right of first refusal to return post-renovation at current rate.

**Risk Level**: HIGH

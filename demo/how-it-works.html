How AfrexAI Works

From signup to results in 5 clear steps. Here's exactly what happens when you onboard.

1

Discovery Call

We learn about your business, workflows, and where AI agents can have the biggest impact. 30 minutes, no commitment.

2

Agent Design

We map your processes to our agent skills and design a custom workforce — typically 3-9 agents tailored to your vertical.

3

Deployment

Agents are configured, connected to your tools (CRM, email, calendar), and deployed. Most setups complete in 48 hours.

4

Monitoring & Tuning

We monitor agent performance, tune prompts, and optimize workflows during a 2-week calibration period.

5

Results & Scale

You see measurable outcomes — leads generated, hours saved, tasks completed. Then we scale what's working.

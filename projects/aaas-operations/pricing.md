## Tier 1: Single Agent — $1,500/month

**Introductory Price: $750/month** (first 3 months, then $1,500/month)

### What's Included
- 1 dedicated AI agent deployed on isolated infrastructure
- Custom persona and behaviour configuration
- Up to 3 integration connections (email, Slack, CRM, etc.)
- 2x daily automated runs (morning + evening cycles)
- 99.5% uptime SLA
- Monthly performance report
- Email support (response within 24 hours)
- Quarterly strategy review (45 min)

### Best For
- Solo operators and small teams
- Businesses wanting to automate one key workflow
- Email management, scheduling, basic outreach
- "Try before you buy" path to full swarm

| **Gross margin at $750 intro** | **$616 (82%)** |

## Sales Guidelines

### Introductory Pricing Rules
- 50% discount for first 3 months only
- Available for Tier 1 only (Single Agent)
- Auto-converts to full price at month 4
- Must be on annual commitment to qualify (can cancel, but no mid-term discount extension)

### Negotiation Floor
- Single Agent: Never below $500/mo (even for strategic accounts)
- Full Swarm: Never below $3,500/mo
- Below floor = needs CEO approval

### Contract Terms
- Month-to-month billing (Stripe subscription)
- 30-day cancellation notice
- Annual contracts available at 10% discount ($16,200/yr single, $54,000/yr swarm)
- Setup fee: waived for first 10 customers, then $500 (single) / $1,500 (swarm)

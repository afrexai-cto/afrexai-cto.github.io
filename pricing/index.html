Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in each plan?
Every plan includes fully managed AI agents deployed to your systems, integrations with your existing tools (email, CRM, ERP), weekly performance reports, and dedicated Slack/Teams support. Higher tiers add more agents, custom integrations, and priority support.
Why do some verticals cost more?
Legal and healthcare verticals require specialized compliance frameworks, domain-specific training data, and additional security measures (HIPAA, SOC 2). Finance includes enhanced audit trails. These premiums cover the real cost of building compliant AI for regulated industries.
Can I add more agents later?
Absolutely. Additional agents beyond your plan are $1,800/mo each. You can scale up at any time ‚Äî we'll deploy new agents within 24 hours of your request.

What if it doesn't work for us?
We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If your agents aren't delivering measurable value within the first month, we'll work with you to reconfigure or provide a full refund. No questions asked.
Is my data secure?

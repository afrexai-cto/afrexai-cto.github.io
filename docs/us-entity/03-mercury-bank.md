# Mercury Bank Account Setup Guide

## Why Mercury?

- Built for startups and tech companies
- Accepts non-US founders (common with Delaware LLCs)
- No monthly fees, no minimum balance
- FDIC insured (via partner banks)
- Clean API, integrates with accounting tools
- Virtual & physical debit cards
- ACH, wires, checks all supported

## Requirements

Before applying, have ready:
- ✅ Certificate of Formation (Delaware)
- ✅ EIN (IRS letter / SS-4 confirmation)
- ✅ Operating Agreement (signed by all members)
- ✅ Government-issued ID for all members (passport works)
- ✅ US business address (virtual office is fine — see [virtual office guide](./04-virtual-office.md))
- ✅ Company website or description of business

## Step-by-Step

### 1. Apply Online
- Go to [mercury.com](https://mercury.com)
- Click "Open an Account"
- Select **LLC** as business type

### 2. Company Information
- **Legal name:** AfrexAI LLC
- **State of formation:** Delaware
- **EIN:** [your EIN]
- **Business address:** [NYC virtual office address]
- **Industry:** Technology / Software / AI
- **Website:** afrexai.com
- **Description:** AI-powered technology platform

### 3. Owner/Member Information
For BOTH members (>25% ownership triggers KYC):
- Full legal name
- Date of birth
- Residential address (UK addresses are fine)
- Passport scan (front + back or info page)
- Role in company (CEO / CTO)
- Ownership percentage (50% each)

### 4. Upload Documents
- Certificate of Formation
- EIN confirmation letter
- Signed Operating Agreement

### 5. Review & Submit
- Mercury reviews within **1–5 business days**
- They may request additional info (respond promptly)
- Common follow-ups: proof of business activity, website, social media

### 6. Account Approved
- Receive account & routing numbers
- Order physical debit cards (shipped to US address)
- Set up virtual cards immediately
- Enable ACH and wire transfers

## Tips for International Founders

- **Use your UK address** for personal details — Mercury accepts international founders
- **Virtual office address** is sufficient for business address
- **Have your website live** before applying — increases approval odds
- **If initially rejected:** email support with more context about your business. Mercury is founder-friendly and often reconsiders
- **Via Firstbase/Stripe Atlas:** if you formed through these, they have direct Mercury partnerships that simplify the process

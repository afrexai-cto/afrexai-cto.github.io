## Executive Summary

The AI agents market is **$7.9B in 2025**, projected to hit **$11.6B in 2026** (45.8% CAGR). Enterprise AI application spending reached **$37B in 2025** (3.2x YoY growth per Menlo Ventures). **Top recommendation:** Focus on **Financial Services** and **Healthcare** as primary verticals, with **Legal** as a high-margin adjacency. Target mid-market enterprises ($500M-$5B revenue) with $150K-$500K initial contracts expanding to $500K-$1M+ annually.

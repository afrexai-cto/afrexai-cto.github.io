## Cost-Saving Tips

1. **Get startup pricing on everything.** Compliance platforms, auditors, and pen test firms all discount for startups.
2. **Start with a small scope.** Only include the systems/services that customers care about. Don't audit your internal wiki.
3. **Skip Type I if you can wait.** Go straight to Type II unless you need a report for an active deal.
4. **Use the compliance platform's auditor marketplace.** Bundled deals are common.
5. **Automate everything.** The more your compliance platform collects automatically, the cheaper the audit (less auditor time).
6. **Consider a newer/smaller audit firm.** Big 4 charges 3‚Äì5x more. Firms like Prescient Assurance, Johanson Group, or A-LIGN offer quality at startup-friendly prices.

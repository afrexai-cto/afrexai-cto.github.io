# Channel Performance Benchmarks & CPL Targets
## AfrexAI Marketing Analyst — Unit Economics for £1M ARR

### Channel Economics Overview

AfrexAI must acquire ~40 clients at £25K ACV to hit £1M ARR. With a 33% close
rate from SQL, that means ~120 SQLs needed. Understanding cost-per-lead by
channel determines where to invest the limited marketing budget.

### Target Unit Economics

| Metric                  | Target           | Rationale                          |
|-------------------------|------------------|------------------------------------|
| Blended CAC             | £1,500-2,000     | Allows 12-month payback at 65% GM  |
| LTV:CAC ratio           | >5:1             | Healthy for B2B services           |
| Blended CPL (MQL)       | £50-100          | Based on 15% MQL→SQL, 33% close   |
| Cost per SQL            | £300-500         | MQL→SQL at 15-20%                  |
| Cost per client         | £1,500-2,000     | SQL→client at 33%                  |
| Payback period          | <12 months       | Critical for cash flow             |

### Channel Performance Dashboard

Track weekly for each channel:
- Spend (£)
- Impressions / Reach
- Leads generated (MQL)
- CPL (£)
- MQL → SQL conversion
- SQL → Client conversion
- Revenue attributed
- ROAS (return on ad spend)

### Optimization Rules

- Kill any paid channel with CPL >2x target for 3 consecutive weeks
- Double down on any channel with CPL <50% of target
- Test new channels quarterly with £500 experiment budget
- Always have 2-3 active A/B tests running across channels
- Review channel mix monthly, major reallocation quarterly

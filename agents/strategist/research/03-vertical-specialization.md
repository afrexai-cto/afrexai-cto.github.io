# Vertical Specialisation — Which Verticals to Target and Why
## AfrexAI Strategist | 2026 UK Market

### Why Verticalise
Horizontal AI agencies compete on everything, win on nothing. Vertical specialists:
- Close 2-3x faster (speak the client's language from day one)
- Command 20-40% price premium (perceived expertise > generalist)
- Generate referrals within the vertical (tight communities)
- Produce reusable IP (same problems repeat across companies in a vertical)

For AfrexAI to hit £1M ARR in 2026, depth in 3 verticals beats breadth in 10.

### Recommended Verticals (Ranked)

## 1. B2B SaaS Companies (20-200 employees) — PRIMARY

**Why this vertical:**
- 6,000+ B2B SaaS companies in the UK (2026 estimate)
- High willingness to pay for automation (tech-forward culture)
- Already use tools AfrexAI integrates with (HubSpot, Slack, Notion, Stripe)
- Understand recurring revenue → comfortable with monthly retainers
- Dense network in London, Manchester, Bristol — word-of-mouth travels fast

**Common pain points:**
- Customer onboarding is manual and inconsistent (takes 2-4 hours per client)
- Lead scoring and routing relies on gut feel, not data
- Churn prediction is reactive — cancellations are surprises
- Internal reporting consumes 10+ hours/week across teams
- Support ticket triage is slow, repetitive, and draining senior engineers

| Automation | Time Saved | Typical ROI |
|---|---|---|
| AI-powered customer onboarding | 15-20 hrs/week | 3-5x in 60 days |

| Automated churn risk alerts | Preventive | £10K+ saved/quarter |
| AI reporting dashboards | 10-15 hrs/week | 2-4x in 30 days |
| Support ticket classification | 5-10 hrs/week | 2x in 45 days |

**Go-to-market:**

## 2. Digital & Marketing Agencies (10-100 employees) — SECONDARY

**Why this vertical:**
- 25,000+ agencies in the UK (DMA estimate)
- Razor-thin margins (15-25%) → desperate for efficiency
- Deliver repetitive services (reporting, content, SEO audits) ripe for AI
- Agencies talk to other agencies — strongest referral network in business
- Low switching cost for tools → will adopt quickly

**Common pain points:**
- Monthly client reporting takes 2-5 hours PER CLIENT
- Content creation bottleneck limits how many clients they can serve
- SEO audits and competitor analysis are manual and slow
- Proposal writing is time-consuming and low-conversion
- Resource allocation across clients is chaotic

## 3. Professional Services (20-200 employees) — TERTIARY

**Why this vertical:**
- Accountancy, legal, consulting, recruitment — massive UK sectors
- High hourly rates (£100-£500/hr) → automating admin has enormous ROI
- Very manual industries despite high revenue (still using spreadsheets + email)
- Regulated environments = value compliance-aware AI (GDPR, FCA, SRA)
- Long client relationships → high LTV once you're in

**Common pain points:**
- Document review and extraction (legal, accounting) consumes junior staff
- Client communication follow-up is inconsistent
- Compliance checks are manual and error-prone
- Knowledge management: expertise lives in people's heads, not systems
- Billing and time tracking has significant leakage

**AfrexAI automations that sell:**
| Automation | Time Saved | Typical ROI |
|---|---|---|
| AI document review/extraction | 10-20 hrs/week | 5-10x in 90 days |
| Automated client follow-up | 5-8 hrs/week | 2-3x in 60 days |
| Compliance monitoring alerts | Preventive | £20K+ risk reduction |
| Knowledge base AI assistant | 3-5 hrs/week | Hard to quantify, high value |
| Smart time tracking + billing | 5-10 hrs/week | 3-5x in 60 days |

**Go-to-market:**
- Partner with accounting software providers (Xero, QuickBooks partners)
- Attend Legal Geek, Accountex, Recruitment Agency Expo

### Decision Framework: When to Add a New Vertical
- ✅ 5+ paying clients in current vertical (proven demand)
- ✅ Reusable automations (>60% of build is templated)
- ✅ At least 2 case studies with measurable ROI
- ✅ Clear channel partner identified in new vertical
- ❌ Don't add if current verticals have <80% capacity utilisation

## Objection Handling Playbook

| Objection | Response Framework |
|---|---|
| "Too expensive" | Reframe as cost of inaction. Show ROI timeline. |
| "We tried AI before" | Ask what specifically failed. Differentiate our approach. |
| "Our team won't adopt it" | Address change management. Show low-friction entry points. |
| "Data security concerns" | Detail our architecture. UK data residency. Certifications. |
| "Not the right time" | Quantify cost of delay per month. Find low-commitment starting point. |
| "Need to talk to [decision maker]" | Offer tailored exec summary. Ask to join next call. |

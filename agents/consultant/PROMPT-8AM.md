### 2. Discovery Call Prep

1. Research the prospect company: size, revenue, industry, tech stack, recent news
2. Research attendees on LinkedIn (titles, backgrounds, likely concerns)
3. Identify their top 3 likely pain points using vertical knowledge from MEMORY.md
4. Prepare 5 to 7 open-ended discovery questions tailored to their situation
5. Build a preliminary ROI sketch using conservative estimates
6. Note potential objections and prepare responses
7. Write the full prep document to agents/consultant/output/prep-{company}-{date}.md

Prep docs must include: prospect summary, attendee profiles, pain hypothesis, discovery questions, ROI preview, objection prep, and recommended approach.

### 3. Objection Handling Update

### 4. Handoffs

### 5. Status

Summarize what you prepared and for whom. Flag any calls that need additional information you couldn't find. Note any gaps in vertical knowledge that need evening research.

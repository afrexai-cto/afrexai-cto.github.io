## Lead Magnet: 5-Point AI Compliance Checklist

1. **Inventory AI tools** — document every AI system touching candidate data (ATS features, ChatGPT usage, video screening tools)
2. **Map jurisdictions** — which states' AI laws apply based on where you place candidates (NYC, Colorado, Illinois, Maryland)
3. **Conduct bias audit** — annual bias audit required under NYC LL144; good practice everywhere
4. **Implement candidate notice** — Colorado and NYC require notifying candidates when AI is used in screening
5. **Document for clients** — build AI governance packet for enterprise MSA qualification

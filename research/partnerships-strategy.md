## 5. White-Label Opportunities

The white-label AI agent market is exploding in 2026. Key findings:

### Market Context

- 1 in 4 companies launched agentic pilots in 2025 (TimeTrex)
- White-label platforms like **Stammer.ai**, **Insighto.ai**, and **Relevance AI** are growing fast
- 2026 is the year pilots go to production at scale

### White-Label Strategy for AfrexAI

**Option A: White-label FOR other agencies**
- Package AfrexAI's OpenClaw agent configurations as white-label solutions
- Other agencies (marketing, web dev, consulting) resell under their brand
- AfrexAI earns recurring platform/support fees
- Target: agencies that want to offer AI but lack technical capability

**Option B: White-label WITH a platform**
- Partner with Stammer.ai or Relevance AI as an implementation agency
- Use their white-label infrastructure, add AfrexAI expertise on top

**Option C: Build a white-label offering on OpenClaw**
- OpenClaw is open-source — create branded agent deployments
- Pre-configured "Agent Workforce Kits" for verticals (law firms, real estate, healthcare)
- Monthly subscription model

### Recommended: Option A + C combined
- Build on OpenClaw (no licensing fees)
- White-label to agencies (scalable revenue)
- This is the highest-margin play

# In-House Counsel AI Implementation Targets
**Created: 2026-02-16 | Vertical: Legal (In-House)**

## Why In-House Counsel NOW
- Above the Law (Feb 13): in-house teams using AI to benchmark outside counsel costs
- Thomson Reuters: org-wide AI adoption doubled to 40%, but only 18% track ROI
- In-house GCs have budget authority AND AI motivation (cost reduction)
- Shorter sales cycle than law firms (1 decision maker vs. partnership vote)

## Target Profile
- **Title:** General Counsel, VP Legal, Chief Legal Officer
- **Company size:** $200M-$2B revenue (big enough to have legal team, small enough to not build in-house)
- **Industry:** Financial services, healthcare, tech, manufacturing (regulated = more legal overhead)
- **Pain:** Rising outside counsel costs, compliance burden, manual contract review
- **Budget:** $120K-$250K for AI implementation

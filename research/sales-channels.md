## 3. Cold Email â€” Deliverability & Best Practices

### Domain & Authentication Setup (Non-Negotiable)
1. **Buy separate domains** for outbound (e.g., afrexai.io, getafrexai.com) â€” never use your primary domain
2. **SPF Record**: Add to DNS â€” authorises which servers can send on your behalf
3. **DKIM**: Enable cryptographic signing for all outbound emails
4. **DMARC**: Start with `p=none` (monitor mode), then move to `p=quarantine` â†’ `p=reject`
5. **Custom tracking domain**: Avoid shared tracking pixels that tank reputation

### Domain Warming Schedule
| Week | Daily Volume | Notes |
|------|-------------|-------|
| 1-2 | 5-10/day | Send to known contacts, get replies |
| 3-4 | 30-50/day | Mix of warm + cold, monitor bounces |
| 5-6 | 80-120/day | Scale gradually, watch spam complaints |
| 7+ | 120-150/day max | Cap per mailbox; add more mailboxes to scale |

### Email Copy Best Practices (2025-2026)
- **Subject line**: Short (3-5 words), no spam triggers, personalised
- **Body**: Under 120 words, plain text (no HTML templates for cold), one clear CTA
- **Personalisation**: Reference specific company challenge, recent news, or tech stack
- **Always include**: Easy opt-out / unsubscribe
- **Spam complaint rate**: Keep under 0.1% (Google/Yahoo enforce this since Feb 2024)
- **Bounce rate**: Keep under 2% â€” verify all lists before sending

### Tools for Deliverability
- **Instantly.ai**: Built-in warmup + inbox rotation
- **Mailpool**: Warmup network
- **ZeroBounce / NeverBounce**: Email verification
- **Mail-tester.com**: Free deliverability scoring

### Multiple Mailbox Strategy
- Use 3-5 mailboxes per domain
- 3-5 domains = 9-25 mailboxes
- 50 emails/day per mailbox = 450-1,250 emails/day total capacity
- Rotate sending across mailboxes automatically

## 7. Community-Led Growth

### Strategy (Based on What's Working in 2025-2026)
Community-led growth (CLG) = build a network of champions who drive awareness, conviction, and referrals. Companies like Figma, dbt Labs, and Notion have proven this model.

### Tactics for AfrexAI
1. **Launch an AI community** (Slack/Discord) for your target audience (e.g., "AI Leaders Africa" or "AI for Enterprise" community)
2. **Host monthly virtual roundtables** â€” invite 10-15 prospects + 2-3 existing clients
3. **Create an "AI Champions" program** â€” train power users who evangelize
4. **Open-source a tool or framework** â€” builds credibility and developer goodwill
5. **Run a podcast** interviewing AI leaders in target industries
6. **Contribute to existing communities**: r/MachineLearning, Hacker News, AI Twitter/X, LinkedIn AI groups

### The 5 Ps Framework for B2B CLG
1. **People**: Who is your community for? (Be specific)
2. **Place**: Where do they already gather? (Meet them there)
3. **Purpose**: What unique value does the community provide?
4. **Participation**: How do you drive consistent engagement?
5. **Pipeline**: How does community activity convert to revenue?

### Measurement
- Community size + growth rate
- Active participation rate (target >20%)
- Leads sourced from community
- Revenue influenced by community members
- NPS/referral rate of community members vs. non-members

## 8. Channel Partner & Reseller Models

### Model Options

**A. Referral Partners (Lightest Touch)**
- Partner refers lead â†’ AfrexAI closes + delivers
- 10-15% commission on first-year revenue
- Best for: consultants, advisors, complementary SaaS companies

**B. Reseller Partners (Medium Investment)**
- Partner sells AfrexAI under their own relationships
- 20-30% margin on resold services
- Partner handles first-line relationship; AfrexAI delivers
- Best for: IT consultancies, system integrators

**C. White-Label Partners (Deepest)**
- Partner rebrands AfrexAI capabilities as their own
- 30-40% margin; AfrexAI provides backend delivery
- Best for: large consultancies wanting to offer "their" AI capability
- Requires significant trust + legal framework

### Building the Program
1. Start with 3-5 referral partners (prove the model)
2. Create partner enablement kit: pitch deck, case studies, demo access, pricing guide
3. Build a simple deal registration system (avoid channel conflict)
4. Monthly partner calls + quarterly business reviews
5. Graduate top referral partners to reseller status

### Target Partner Profiles
- **Africa**: Local IT consultancies, digital transformation firms, telecom value-added services
- **UK**: Boutique management consultancies, technology advisors, innovation agencies
- **Global**: Cloud provider partner ecosystems (AWS/Azure/GCP), Salesforce SI partners

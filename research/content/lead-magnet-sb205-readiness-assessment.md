## THE ASSESSMENT

### Colorado SB-205 AI Compliance Readiness Score

**Instructions:** Score each item 0 (not started), 1 (in progress), or 2 (complete). Total your score at the end.

#### Section 1: AI System Inventory (Max 10 points)
- [ ] We have a complete inventory of all AI systems in production ___
- [ ] Each system is classified as high-risk or standard under SB-205 criteria ___
- [ ] We know which systems make or influence "consequential decisions" ___
- [ ] Third-party AI tools (vendors, APIs) are included in our inventory ___
- [ ] Each system has a designated internal owner ___

#### Section 2: Algorithmic Impact Assessments (Max 10 points)
- [ ] We've completed AIAs for all high-risk systems ___
- [ ] AIAs include bias testing across protected classes ___
- [ ] AIAs document data sources, training methodology, and known limitations ___
- [ ] AIAs are reviewed and updated at least annually ___
- [ ] Results are available for regulatory inspection ___

#### Section 3: Consumer Notification & Transparency (Max 10 points)
- [ ] Consumers are notified when AI influences decisions about them ___
- [ ] Notifications explain what data the AI uses ___
- [ ] Consumers can request human review of AI decisions ___
- [ ] Opt-out mechanisms exist where required ___
- [ ] Notification language is clear, not buried in ToS ___

#### Section 4: Governance Framework (Max 10 points)
- [ ] Written AI governance policy exists ___
- [ ] Roles and responsibilities are assigned (AI ethics lead, compliance) ___
- [ ] Regular monitoring for bias, drift, and accuracy is in place ___
- [ ] Incident response plan exists for AI failures/errors ___
- [ ] Board or C-suite receives regular AI risk reports ___

#### Section 5: Documentation & Audit Trail (Max 10 points)
- [ ] All AI decisions in regulated areas are logged ___
- [ ] Logs include inputs, outputs, and model version ___
- [ ] Retention policy meets regulatory requirements ___
- [ ] Documentation is sufficient for a regulatory audit ___
- [ ] We can reproduce any past AI decision if challenged ___

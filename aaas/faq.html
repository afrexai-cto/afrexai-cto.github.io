Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about AfrexAI's AI-as-a-Service

General
What is AI-as-a-Service (AaaS)?
AaaS is AfrexAI's managed AI agent service. We deploy purpose-built AI agents that integrate directly into your business operations — handling tasks like customer support, data analysis, scheduling, and workflow automation — so your team can focus on high-value work.
How does AaaS work?
We start with a discovery call to understand your workflows and pain points. From there, we configure and deploy a dedicated AI agent (or swarm of agents) tailored to your needs. Agents connect to your existing tools via APIs and are monitored and optimised continuously by our team.
What industries do you serve?
AfrexAI works across a wide range of industries including professional services, e-commerce, healthcare administration, real estate, fintech, and SaaS. If your business has repetitive processes that consume staff time, our agents can help.
Do I need technical expertise to use AaaS?
Not at all. We handle all the technical setup, integration, and ongoing management. You interact with your agent through familiar channels — Slack, email, dashboards — and our team is always available for support.

Security & Privacy
How is my data kept secure?
Every client gets a fully isolated environment. Your data is never shared across accounts or used to train models. All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3).
Are you SOC 2 compliant?
We are actively pursuing SOC 2 Type II certification and follow SOC 2 controls across our infrastructure today — including access management, audit logging, and incident response procedures.
Can I control what data the AI agent can access?
Absolutely. You define the data boundaries during onboarding. Agents operate on a least-privilege basis and only access the systems and data you explicitly authorise.
Where is my data stored?
Data is stored on SOC 2-compliant cloud infrastructure with data residency options available. We can accommodate region-specific requirements upon request.

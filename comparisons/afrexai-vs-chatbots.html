AI Agents vs Chatbots: What's the Difference?

Your chatbot answers questions. An AI agent actually does the work. Here's why that distinction matters for your business.

Chatbots React. Agents Act.

A chatbot waits for input and follows a script. An AI agent understands goals, makes decisions, and executes tasks autonomously.

Traditional Chatbots

  • Reactive — waits for user input
  • Scripted decision trees
  • Single channel (usually web widget)
  • Can't handle complex or multi-step tasks
  • Breaks when users go off-script
  • No memory between sessions
  • Deflects to humans constantly

AfrexAI Agents

  • Proactive — initiates actions on its own
  • Autonomous reasoning and decision-making
  • Multi-channel: email, phone, chat, SMS
  • Handles complex, multi-step workflows
  • Adapts to unexpected situations
  • Learns and improves over time
  • Actually completes the work end-to-end

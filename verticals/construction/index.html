The Problem

Construction runs on paper, phone calls, and guesswork

Site Reporting Is a Time Sink

Foremen spend hours after long days writing up site reports. Details get lost, reports arrive late, and project managers chase updates instead of managing.

Bid Estimation Is Manual & Error-Prone

Estimators juggle spreadsheets, historical data, and gut feel. Underbid and you lose money. Overbid and you lose the job.

Project Tracking Falls Through Cracks

With multiple sites, subcontractors, and timelines, progress tracking becomes reactive — you find out about delays after they've already cost you.

The Solution

AI agents built for the way construction actually works

Your crews are on-site, not at desks. These agents meet them where they are.

  • Voice-to-Report Agent (SiteVoice) — Speak into your phone on-site, get a formatted daily report in minutes. No typing, no templates, no delays.

  • Progress Tracking Agent — Aggregates updates from all sites, flags delays, and generates weekly dashboards automatically

Getting Started

What exactly is an "AI agent workforce"?

Think of AI agents as digital employees that handle specific tasks autonomously. Unlike chatbots that just answer questions, agents actually do work — they process documents, send follow-ups, reconcile data, coordinate between systems, and make routine decisions. They run 24/7, don't take sick days, and cost a fraction of a human employee.

How is this different from ChatGPT or other AI tools?

ChatGPT is a conversation tool — you ask it things and it responds. AI agents are autonomous workers that take actions in your business systems. They connect to your CRM, email, accounting software, and databases. They don't wait for prompts — they monitor, execute, and report. It's the difference between asking someone a question and hiring someone to do the job.

How long does it take to get started?

Our "Week I Win" guarantee means you see a working AI agent demo in 7 days. Full deployment typically takes 2-4 weeks depending on complexity. Compare that to 6-18 months with traditional consulting firms. We start with the highest-ROI workflow and expand from there.

What if it doesn't work for my business?

We offer a "$40K Recovery" guarantee — if our AI agents don't save or generate at least $40,000 in value within 90 days, we keep working until they do. We also start with a discovery call to make sure there's a genuine fit before either side commits.

Security & Compliance

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. Agents operate within your existing infrastructure — we don't move your data to our servers. We follow SOC 2 principles, and for regulated industries (healthcare, financial services, legal), we build compliance directly into agent workflows.

Do AI agents have access to everything?

No. We implement principle of least privilege — each agent only accesses the specific systems and data it needs to do its job. You approve all integrations, and everything is auditable. You can revoke access at any time.

What about HIPAA / SOX / GDPR compliance?

We build compliance into the agent architecture from day one. For healthcare (HIPAA), we ensure PHI handling follows required safeguards. For financial services (SOX), we maintain audit trails and access controls. For any business handling EU data (GDPR), we implement data minimization and right-to-deletion capabilities.

Technical Details

What systems do AI agents integrate with?

We integrate with virtually any business system that has an API or accepts email — CRMs (Salesforce, HubSpot), accounting (QuickBooks, Xero), practice management (Clio, PracticePanther), property management (AppFolio, Buildium), EHRs, ERPs, and more. If your system has an API, we can connect to it.

Do I need to change my existing software?

No. AI agents work alongside your existing tools — they don't replace them. Think of them as a new team member that already knows how to use all your software. No migration, no retraining your staff on new systems.

What happens when an agent doesn't know what to do?

Agents are built with clear escalation paths. When they encounter something outside their training — an unusual edge case, an ambiguous request, or a high-stakes decision — they escalate to a human with full context. Over time, they learn from these escalations and handle more autonomously.

Can I see what the agents are doing?

Yes. Every agent action is logged and auditable. We provide dashboards showing what each agent processed, decisions it made, and outcomes. Full transparency — these agents work for you, not the other way around.

The Challenge

SiteVoice, a mid-market construction management firm, ran into the same problem plaguing the industry: project managers drowning in paperwork. On a typical $2.8M commercial build, the PM spent 6+ hours daily compiling site reports, chasing subcontractor documentation, updating schedules, and generating client progress updates.

Delays in documentation cascaded into scheduling conflicts, missed inspections, and cost overruns. On their last three projects, documentation-related delays averaged $38K in additional costs per project.

The Solution

Site Report Agent ‚Äî Ingests daily photos, voice memos from foremen, and IoT sensor data to auto-generate comprehensive daily site reports with weather conditions, workforce counts, material deliveries, and progress percentages.
